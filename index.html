Our Story

Baked with
Generations of Love

What started as a small home kitchen in Model Town, Ludhiana has grown into the city's most trusted name in sweets and baking. Founded by Sardar Gurmeet Singh in 2008, Mithas has been feeding joy to families across Punjab for over 15 years.

We source only the finest ingredients — pure desi ghee, farm-fresh dairy, and Belgian couverture chocolate — because we believe you deserve nothing less than the best.

"Mithas isn't just a shop — it's where memories are made, celebrations begin, and every sweet moment is honoured."
